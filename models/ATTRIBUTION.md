## 3dbus/ (Generic Bus)

**Source:** https://opengameart.org/content/3d-bus  
**Author:** ajanhallinta  
**License:** CC0 (Public Domain)  
**Files:** BUS.obj, BUS.mtl  

A detailed bus model with 7,352 vertices and 5,669 faces.
No textures used, only materials. Does not have interior.

## vehicle-assets/ (Minibus)

**Source:** https://opengameart.org/content/vehicles-assets-pt1  
**Author:** eracoon  
**License:** CC0 (Public Domain)  
**Files:** car-minibus-{color}.obj, car-minibus-{color}.mtl  

Low poly minibus models in 7 colors: blue, citrus, green, orange, red, silver, violet.
Part of a larger vehicle asset pack with 84 different vehicles.
Website: http://www.racoon-media.nl

---

## lowpoly-vehicles/ (Low Poly Vehicles)

**Source:** https://opengameart.org/content/low-poly-vehicles-pack  
**Author:** Daniel Quevedo  
**License:** CC0 (Public Domain)  
**Files:** Bus.fbx, Sedan.fbx, Pickup.fbx, Jeep.fbx, Wagon.fbx  

Low poly vehicle pack including bus and other city vehicles.
Only materials with colors included - no textures.
