## Generic Bus (3dbus/)

**Source:** https://opengameart.org/content/3d-bus  
**Author:** ajanhallinta  
**License:** CC0 (Public Domain)  
**Files:** BUS.obj, BUS.mtl  

Description:
A detailed bus model with 7,352 vertices and 5,669 faces.
No textures used, only materials. Does not have interior.
Made entirely using Blender.

**Source:** https://opengameart.org/content/lowpoly-public-transport
